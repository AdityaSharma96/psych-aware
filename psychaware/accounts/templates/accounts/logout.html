About Us

Psych Aware is a platform dedicated to everyone’s favourite subject : Ourselves
PSYCH AWARE basically means awareness of the mind. Everyone struggles at times. Psych Aware believes that with the right support, anyone is capable of healing, growth, and change. Whether you’re interested in seeking help or you provide them, you’ve come to the right place.
